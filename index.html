We invite drug discovery and development SMEs and startups to join our next phase of product testing and development at IIT-Kanpur. Funding is available for:

  • Advanced testing of our triple agonist anti‑obesity medication
  • Further development of our AI/ML platform including expanding to new diseases
  • Pre‑clinical validation studies

Scientific Validation

  • Proof‑of‑concept presented at Global Healthcare Innovation Consortium, Carle Illinois College of Medicine
  • Triple agonist with EC50 superior to best‑in‑class therapies
  • Performance validated through advanced docking studies

What will be my role?

Your support is needed for:

  • Platform testing and feedback with real‑world data and projects
  • Commercial deployment practices (on‑prem/cloud, security protocols)
  • Support In Vitro/In Vivo pre‑clinical testing at your site or IIT‑K labs

What is in it for me?

Funding through PRIP will help you:

  • Commercialize platform into global markets
  • Gain access to talent and state‑of‑the‑art labs
  • Get priority rights and platform support for your projects
